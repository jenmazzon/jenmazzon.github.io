Join the Quest for Social Impact

Tech has finally hit the social impact sector with a bang. Non-profits, government agencies, B-corps and those rare mission-driven for profits are all looking to solve problems that matter at scale. Philanthropic foundations, billionaires and governments are putting $$$$ into innovative social impact organizations. Social entrepreneurs are joining fellowships, incubators and competitions to get their social impact ventures funded. Do you want to be a social entrepreneur? YOU CAN BE ONE.
The $$$$ people understand that they need tech folks to join in for problems that matter to get solved at scale, There are many open tech jobs in social impact organizations. Do you want to to have a tech job in social impact? YOU CAN GET ONE.

Advance Your Career in Tech

You love building tech products. You want to hone your craft, deepen your skills, and make a difference.
